The Story

A myriad of scientific studies have demonstrated the importance of sleep in improving mental health, strengthening memory and healthy brain function, preventing weight gain, and more, but why do 84 million adults (35% of the population) still report getting less than 7 hours of sleep each day?

Especially in college, the stigma of sleep along with the institutional factors of alternating class schedules, 7 AM assignment deadlines, and required digital screen use, can make sleep deprivation almost inevitable.

This is why I created DreamOn: a mobile sleep leaderboard that gamifies sleep and lets you track daily performance so you can see how the power of sleep personally affects you.

Sleep is not for the weak; sleep is for everyone.
I hope DreamOn can help you get the Z's you need.
